An inland terminal, also defined as a 'dry port', is a critical node of a supply chain that enables the redistribution of the flow of goods arriving from seaports. AD Mark Logistics' network of inland terminal facilities located at the country's key gateway locations significantly boosts our integrated logistics service portfolio.

Our strategically located Container Freight Station (CFS) at Panvel connects the country's key seaports to manufacturing hubs and consumption centers across India. Together, they hold the capacity to handle over 120,000 TEUs per annum.

Our highly skilled and experienced in-house construction and design teams take care of the development, operation, and management of terminals to guarantee seamless supply chain operations.

Our inland terminal infrastructure helps our team of logistics experts provide a wide range of services including customs clearance, cargo survey, custom examination, list on/off, port clearance, stuffing/de-stuffing, consolidation, FCL (Full Container Load), LCL (Less-Than Container Load), and international logistics, among others.

CFS

CFS Panvel, Maharashtra

The Container Freight Station (CFS) at Panvel, Navi Mumbai caters to the container traffic at Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT). It is growing exponentially every year with a current capacity of handling 120,000 TEUs per annum. Strategically located on the outskirts of Panvel, with close connectivity to the city through the Mumbai-Pune Expressway, the CFS facilitates hassle-free movement of inbound and outbound cargo to and from Mumbai.
