The Digital Nervous System

A supply chain control tower is, at its core, an information and data analysis center that helps you make smart decisions. It acts as a digital nervous system, integrating disparate data silos from manufacturers, retailers, and distributors into a synchronized, resilient operation.

Today, global organizations use a control tower to orchestrate the movement of goods from source to destination, preventing and minimizing risks through high-frequency telemetry.
